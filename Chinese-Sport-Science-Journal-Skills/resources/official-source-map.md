# Official Source Map — Chinese Sport Science (体育学) journals

This file gives one official-source starting query per journal profile. It is a
routing aid, not a cached copy of any live guideline. Always open the live
official journal site, sponsor / host-institution page, official editorial /
submission system, or the official CNKI journal page before giving
submission-ready instructions.

Routing-map check date: 2026-06-19. For these Chinese journals, start from the
journal's official site, its host sport-university or society page, its official
submission system, or its official CNKI journal page such as
`https://navi.cnki.net/knavi/`; do not treat third-party manuscript agencies as
sources.
