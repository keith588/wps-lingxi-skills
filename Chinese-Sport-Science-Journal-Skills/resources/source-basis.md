# Source Basis and Official-Check Notes

This pack encodes journal-fit and house-style heuristics for the **12 CSSCI
source journals of sport science (体育学, 2025–2026)**. It promotes sport science
from an "Other" entry inside `Chinese-SocialScience-Journal-Skills` to its own
first-class discipline bundle. It is a positioning and re-framing aid, **not** a
static substitute for any journal's live author instructions.

For per-journal starting queries, use `official-source-map.md` in this directory.

## What this bundle covers (and why these venues)

The CSSCI 体育学 source-journal list (2025–2026) contains 12 titles. This bundle
gives each one a self-contained skill:

- **Discipline flagships (society / national-institute system).** 《体育科学》
  (China Sport Science), the China Sport Science Society flagship and the
  field-defining venue; and 《中国体育科技》 (China Sport Science and Technology),
  the more application- and competition-oriented title from the same China
  Institute of Sport Science (国家体育总局体育科学研究所) system.
- **Comprehensive sport-university journals.** The学报 of Beijing, Shanghai,
  Chengdu, Wuhan, Xi'an, Shenyang and Tianjin sport universities — each broadly
  comprehensive across sport science, with house tilts noted in the skills (e.g.
  Chengdu's strength in sport history / ethnic traditional sport; Tianjin's tilt
  toward exercise/human movement science; Shanghai's social-science strength).
- **Humanities / social-science leaning.** 《体育学刊》 (school PE and sport
  humanities, South China Normal University), 《体育学研究》 (sport social science
  and theory, Nanjing Sport Institute), and 《体育与科学》 (reflective,
  interdisciplinary, theory-forward sport studies, Jiangsu).

## Stable, public facts used for fit (no volatile numbers)

Only durable structural conventions inform fit: the host institution / sponsor
of each title; whether a venue is comprehensive or tilts toward 运动人体科学
(exercise physiology, biochemistry, biomechanics, sports medicine, fitness &
health) versus 体育人文社会学 (sport sociology, management, economics, policy,
history & philosophy, school PE, training & competitive sport, ethnic traditional
sport); the dual natural-science / social-science methodological tradition of the
field; and the ethics / human-subjects / reporting expectations that apply to the
natural-science path. CSSCI 体育学 membership itself is treated as a stable
positioning fact, but is **re-verifiable** against the current CSSCI list.

## What each profile deliberately does NOT assert

To stay correct over time, every profile **omits volatile facts** and defers them
to the official-submission checklist instead of stating a number:

- No impact factors, CiteScores, acceptance rates, or "ranked #N" claims.
- No ISSNs, exact word/figure limits, APC amounts, column names, or editor names.
- No fabricated citations (literatures are referred to generically).

## Operational rule for every journal skill

1. Before submitting, search the **live official site or submission system** for
   "《期刊名》 投稿须知 / 征稿简则 / 作者指南" and follow the current version.
2. Trust the journal's official site, its sponsor / host institution page, its
   official editorial/submission system, or its official CNKI journal page
   (e.g. `https://navi.cnki.net/knavi/`) over any third-party manuscript-agency copy.
3. Re-check article-type and length expectations, abstract / keywords / 中图分类号
   format, blind-review and author-info-page requirements, funding disclosure, and
   reference style.
4. For the natural-science path, re-check ethics review / informed consent, trial
   registration where applicable, and data / supplementary-material requirements;
   for the social-science path, re-check whether survey instruments / interview
   protocols must be provided.
5. If the live official instructions conflict with a skill, the official
   instructions win.
